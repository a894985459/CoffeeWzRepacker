## Roadmap / 未來計畫

## Notes / 說明
This document outlines future development directions; its contents may be subject to change depending on actual development progress and do not represent the final order of implementation or a complete list of features.  
本文件列出未來開發方向，內容可能會依實際開發狀況調整，並不代表最終實作順序或完整功能清單。  

## Development direction / 開發方向
- Improve stability  
  提升穩定性  
- Improve UI/UX  
  改善 UI/UX  
- Expand editing features  
  擴充編輯功能  
- Improving performance and maintainability  
  提升效能與可維護性  

## Design Philosophy / 設計理念

- Prioritize stability and data accuracy  
  優先確保系統穩定性與資料正確性 
- Avoid overly complex architectural designs  
  避免過度複雜的架構設計  
- Prioritising modularity and scalability  
  模組化與可擴充性優先  
- Reduce future technical debt  
  減少未來技術債
